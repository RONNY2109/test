Alfa Romeo Giulia EV

The Alfa Romeo Giulia EV, or electric version of the Giulia, will be launched in 2026, maintaining its sedan silhouette despite the popularity of SUVs. It will be one of two new electric vehicles in the D-segment that Alfa Romeo will launch in 2025 and 2026, along with an electric successor to the Stelvio. The Giulia EV is expected to have a range of up to 800 kilometers, with ultra-fast charging capabilities.
